Eligibility requirements

Enrolled or accepted at an accredited U.S. college, university, trade, or vocational school
U.S. citizen, permanent resident, or DACA recipient
Demonstrated financial need
Funds used exclusively for educational expenses
No minimum GPA required for need-based awards
Not related to ScholarPath board members or staff
