Where your money goes

Three funds, one mission

Choose which fund your gift supports, or let it go where it's needed most. Every fund targets a specific group of students who are underserved and underestimated.

First-Generation Student Fund

For students who are the first in their family to attend college. Covers tuition gaps, books, and the living expenses that can derail an otherwise capable student.

Awards up to $2,500

STEM Scholars Fund

For underrepresented students pursuing science, technology, engineering, or mathematics. STEM degrees open doors — this fund helps keep them open for students who'd otherwise be shut out.

Awards up to $2,000

Arts & Humanities Fund

For students pursuing film, music, design, literature, and the humanities. Creative fields have always been harder to fund — this is our answer to that gap.

Awards up to $1,500

How it works

Simple. Transparent. Student-first.

01

You give

Donate any amount through our secure Zeffy form below. Zeffy covers all platform fees, so 100% of your gift reaches students.

02

Students apply

Students at HBCUs, HSIs, and community colleges submit applications with their story, financial need, and goals. Every application is reviewed by a real person.

03

Funds go directly to schools

Award funds are disbursed directly to each student's educational institution — for tuition, books, or other approved expenses. No cash, no middlemen.
